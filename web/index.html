About AeroSense

AeroSense is an air quality monitoring system that uses MQ135 gas sensors and DHT11 temperature/humidity sensors to provide real-time environmental data. The system helps you monitor indoor air quality, alerting you to potentially harmful conditions while tracking temperature and humidity levels.
